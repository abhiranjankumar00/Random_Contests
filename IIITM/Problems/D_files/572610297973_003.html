Problem
You are given a set of cities(named 0 to N-1) and set of roads connecting them. Cities are connected with bidrectional roads. Due to rain all the roads are broken. The cost of fixing each road depends on its length ( 1 cost unit per km ). You want to minimize the cost of fixing roads. For that you fix only few roads, such that all cities are still conneted and total cost of fixing roads is minimum.
First line of input is number of test cases T (1<=T<=100). First line of each test case consist of 2 integers N(number of cities) and M(number of roads). Next M lines has 3 integers U V D,city U and V are connected with a road of distance D. Output T lines with minimum cost of fixing roads.

Constraints :
1<=N<=100000
1<=M<=100000
0<=U,V<=N-1
1<=D<=100000

Input :
1
4 4
0 1 1
1 2 1
2 3 1
3 1 2
Output :
3
Time limit : 1 sec
